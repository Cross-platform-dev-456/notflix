# Notflix User Tutorial

**Your Guide to Discovering Movies & TV Shows**

## Welcome to Notflix! üé¨

Notflix is a movie and TV show discovery app inspired by Netflix.

**What you can do:**
- Browse movies and TV shows by genre
- Search for your favorite content
- Watch trailers
- Create personalized watch lists
- Track what you've watched

## Getting Started

**First Steps:**
1. Launch the Notflix app
2. Browse content immediately (no account needed)
3. Create an account to unlock personalized features:
   - Watch Later list
   - Recently Watched tracking
   - Personalized recommendations

## Home Screen Overview

The home screen is your main browsing hub:

**Features:**
- **Hero Movie**: Featured content at the top
- **Genre Rows**: Horizontal scrolling rows organized by genre
- **Category Filter**: Switch between Movies, TV Shows, or All
- **Genre Filter**: Filter by specific genres

## Browsing Content

**Using Category Filters:**
1. Tap the **Categories** dropdown at the top
2. Select:
   - **All**: Shows both movies and TV shows
   - **Movies**: Shows only movies
   - **TV Shows**: Shows only TV shows

**Using Genre Filters:**
1. Tap the **Genres** dropdown
2. Select a genre (e.g., Action, Comedy, Drama)
3. Content updates to show only that genre

## Navigating Content Rows

**How to browse:**
- **Swipe left/right** on any genre row to see more content
- Each card shows:
  - Movie/TV show poster
  - Title
  - Release date
  - Rating (vote average)

**Tap any card** to view details!

## Search Functionality

**Accessing Search:**
1. Tap the **search icon** (üîç) in the top-right corner
2. Type your search query in the search bar

**Search Features:**
- Results appear in a grid layout
- Tap any result to view details
- Clear search to return to recommendations

## Search Screen - When Logged In

**Personalized Sections:**
- **"Because You Watched"**: Similar content based on your viewing history
- **"Upcoming Movies"**: Latest releases

**Note:** Personalized recommendations appear only when you're logged in!

## Viewing Details

**From any movie/TV show card:**
1. Tap the card to open the details screen
2. View:
   - Large poster or trailer (if available)
   - Overview/description
   - Watch list buttons
   - Episodes (for TV shows)

## Watching Trailers

**On the Details Screen:**
- If a trailer is available, it plays automatically at the top
- Use YouTube controls:
  - Play/Pause
  - Fullscreen
  - Volume control

**Note:** Not all content has trailers available.

## TV Show Episodes

**For TV Shows:**
1. Scroll down on the details screen
2. Find the **Episodes** section
3. Use the **Season dropdown** to switch seasons
4. Each episode shows:
   - Episode number and title
   - Air date
   - Runtime
   - Description

## Creating an Account

**Why create an account?**
- Save movies to Watch Later
- Track Recently Watched
- Get personalized recommendations

**How to sign up:**
1. Tap the **person icon** (üë§) in the top-right
2. Tap **Sign Up** (if you don't have an account)
3. Enter:
   - Username
   - Email
   - Password
   - Confirm password
4. Tap **Sign Up**

## Logging In

**To log in:**
1. Tap the **person icon** (üë§) in the top-right
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **Log In**

**You'll stay logged in** until you manually log out.

## Watch Lists

**Two types of lists:**

1. **Watch Later**
   - Save content to watch later
   - Access from Profile page

2. **Recently Watched**
   - Automatically tracks what you view
   - Used for personalized recommendations

**To add to lists:**
- On any details screen, use the **Watch Later** or **Mark as Watched** buttons

## Profile Page

**Accessing your profile:**
1. Tap the **person icon** (üë§) when logged in
2. View your:
   - **Watch Later** list
   - **Recently Watched** list

**Managing lists:**
- Browse your saved content
- Tap any item to view details
- Remove items from the details screen

## Logging Out

**To log out:**
1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Tap the **logout icon** (üö™) in the top-right
3. Confirm logout

**Note:** You'll lose access to personalized features until you log back in.

## Tips & Tricks

**Best Practices:**
- **Explore genres**: Try different categories to discover new content
- **Use search**: Find specific movies or TV shows quickly
- **Save favorites**: Add to Watch Later for easy access
- **Check ratings**: Higher vote averages indicate popular content

## Troubleshooting

**Common Issues:**

**Content not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a moment and try again

**Can't find a movie?**
- Try different search terms
- Check spelling
- Some content may not be available

**Trailer not playing?**
- Not all content has trailers
- Check your internet connection

**Support Resources:**

- Report issues through the app
